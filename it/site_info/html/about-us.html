About Us

Our Mission Statement

Skillsapien.com is a site to connect recruiters to skilled professionals via focusing on the value of skills and bringing a market mechanism to determine the right value. This is not a traditional job-posting site.

Candidates will be able to post their skill profiles online and have recruiters bid, yes bid, their skills up to a level acceptable for employment. Recruiters will be able to save significant time and costs in querying through structured skill searches and have upfront visibility in candidate rates, with even the possibility to agree prices before the first interview! (thereby saving potentially significant negotiation time and effort)

In short, we aim to align market supply & demand forces directly to work for our candidate’s skills and our recruiting client’s staffing requirements, while in the process revolutionise the way labour skills are valued in a marketplace, forever!

Contact Us

Skillsapien.com is initially aimed at recruiters and IT contractors who has cleared defined marketable skills. Eventually our services will be expanded to provide services for businesses to create an end-to-end value chain for recruiters to help lower their cost to recruit contractors for the vacancies they have.
